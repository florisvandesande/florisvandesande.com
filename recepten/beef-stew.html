Beef stew

ingrediënten

2 lb. beef chuck or round, cut into two-inch stew cubes
2 tablespoons lard
6 shallots, sliced
1 yellow onion, sliced
8 cloves garlic, minced or pressed
1 tablespoons tomato paste
1 cup tomato puree or 2 chopped tomatoes
1 1/2 cups red wine
1 cup beef stock
2 anchovy fillets, minced (optional)
2 bay leaves
3 tablespoons dried herbs de Provence
2 cups yellow beets, peeled and cut in 1 to 2 inch chunks
2 cups carrots peeled and cut into 1 to 2 inch chunks
4 strips orange zest
fresh parsley for garnish, chopped

aanwijzingen

If you're short on time skip to step 2. In a medium pan over medium heat cook lard and onions. Cook, stirring, until softened, about 10 minutes. Add minced garlic cloves and cook for one more minute.
Combine all ingredients except for the fresh parsley into slow cooker. Make sure the liquid completely covers the beef - add more wine or stock if necessary.
Cook on low for 4-6 hours. Serve the stew, and garnish with the fresh parsley.
