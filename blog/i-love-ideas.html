I Love Ideas

I love ideas I honestly do. I write them down in a few short sentences and put them in nvALT and move on. Sometimes I come back to them, add a bit more information or a thought; making it a better,  richer and more realistic idea. But most times, ideas stay just that: ideas. And thats my dilemma. I love ideas, but most of the time, I don't do anything with them (or when I do, I tend not to finish them...). They just sit there, waiting. That's why I have this one idea to change that. To make more ideas a reality. To help other (and myself) go past that little note. I wanna do it, I'm gonna do it. I have to do it.
