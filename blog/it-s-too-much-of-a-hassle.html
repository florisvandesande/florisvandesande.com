It's Too Much of a Hassle

It is, and always will be, a dream of mine to run my own company and be good at it.

The problem is, right now I don't have the time or drive to pursue this dream. I'm working full-time at another company, Grasveld Tuin- en Landschapsarchitecten, where I'm happy and do what I want to do. It's a great job to have; I love it.

And because of this, I don't want to worry about my own company, ruimtegever, for which I don't have the time. It's a loss leader, only costing me time and money, because I'm not investing enough into it. So as of today I'm back to just having a side hobby.

Maybe some day, things will change again. For now, I'm happy.

PS. I'm still available for gigs, but it will be for fun, not money this time.
