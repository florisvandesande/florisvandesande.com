Socially Digital

I'm an old and grumpy man. I don't like the fast paced world we live in today.

I have a smartphone, an information phone, but for me, it's primary function isn't communication. The primary function of my smartphone is to consume, explore and create. Communication is a shamefull part of it.

When one of my friends, or my parents for that matter, texts me, I probably won't reply immediatly, and probably never.

My mind is stupid.

I don't like digital interactions. I don't care for messaging, facebook or twitter. I don't want them in my life. And because of it, I am a bad person...

My friends know I am socially awkward.

When they really want my attention, they call me. I don't like that either, but I probably won't ignore them.

My mind is a giant ball of hurt up, I can't remember anything. I can't remember anything I have to do, what I did or what I was supposed to do.

Is this normal?
