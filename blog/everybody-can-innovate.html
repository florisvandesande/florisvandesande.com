Everybody Can Innovate

These Chinese inventors and entrepreneurs show us that everybody can innovate. No matter who you are or where you com from. Some come from a personal need, some with an eye for profit and others just for the fun of it.

“innovation isn’t just making or adding something new, innovation is making things simpler.”

Alan Taylor made a great collection of Chinese inventions, from Homemade welding masks to can’t-possibly-fly airplanes. I can’t decide which is the coolest, can you?
